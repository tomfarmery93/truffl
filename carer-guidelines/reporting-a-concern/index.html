Reporting a concern

If something doesn't feel right, tell us. Whether it's an owner, a dog, or a situation that worried you, we'd always rather know.

safety@trufflpets.com
  • Tell us what happened in your own words. Detail helps.
  • We investigate every concern urgently and handle it sensitively, in confidence unless you tell us otherwise.

You won't get in trouble for raising something in good faith. That's exactly what we want you to do.
