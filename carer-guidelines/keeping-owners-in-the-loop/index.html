Carer guidelines

Keeping owners in the loop

A little communication goes a long way. Owners relax when they can see their dog is having a good time.

On walks

GPS tracks the route live, so owners can follow along. Just head out and do your thing. The route saves automatically when you're done.

Daycare and boarding

Send a check-in each day with a photo and a quick note on how the dog's doing: eating, toileting, mood. At the start of a stay, a quick "arrived and settling in" message puts an owner at ease. For boarding, an update each evening is plenty, but flag anything that's off straight away rather than waiting for the daily.

Daycare and boarding check-ins are built right into your booking — you'll be prompted for the ones that are due. Open your dashboard to post one.
