Our story

Meet Flick.
The reason all of this exists.

For years, we tried everything to find Flick a consistent, reliable walker — someone who'd actually care. We cycled through independent walkers, small businesses, apps. Some were fine. Most weren't.

The ones we trusted eventually fell through: a move, a schedule change, back to square one. We finally found someone great. Then we moved apartment. She couldn't service our new area.

We were starting over again. And we'd been at this for years.

Flick · Springer Spaniel

The problem we're solving

That problem shouldn't exist. So we decided to fix it.

Truffl Pets is built on one idea: that pet owners deserve to actually trust who's looking after their animals — and to know, in real time, that everything is fine.

Every carer on our platform is interviewed, identity checked, police checked, and observed before they ever take a solo booking. Every walk is GPS tracked. Every visit comes with a photo update.

No more wondering. No more hoping for the best.

"We finally found someone great. Then we moved apartment. She couldn't service our new area. We were starting over again."

That experience — repeated by thousands of pet owners across Sydney — is exactly why Truffl exists. Not as another place to find a walker. As a platform where quality is the only option.

We partner with vets and industry experts to keep our standards current, and we offer ongoing training to all of our carers in animal handling, breed-specific care, and pet first aid.
